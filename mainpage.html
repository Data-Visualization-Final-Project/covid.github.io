World Map of Total Cases

First of all, we depict the number of worldwide COVID-19 total cases and total deaths for each country on the map,
using color intensity. If you click on any country, you can see two other charts, namely the waffle chart and a line chart
when we want to show "Part of a whole" we are using a pie chart.in other words,
A pie chart (or a circle chart) is a circular statistical graphic
that is divided into slices to illustrate numerical proportions.
when we want to show "Correlation" we are using a Bubble chart.
in other words, A bubble chart is a variation of a scatter chart
in which the data points are replaced with bubbles,
and an additional dimension of the data is represented
in the size of the bubbles.
when we want to show "Ranking" we are using a Bar chart.
in other words, A bar chart presents categorical data
with rectangular bars with heights or lengths proportional
to the values that they represent.
The bars can be plotted vertically or horizontally.
A bar graph shows comparisons among discrete categories.
One axis of the chart shows the specific categories
being compared, and the other axis
represents a measured value.there are
four types of barchart : Vertical, Horizontal, Grouped and Stacked Barchart.
